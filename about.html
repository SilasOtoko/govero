This is Holly Govero.


I am a deaf stay at home mom with 5 children. Dont be afraid to talk to me just because I am deaf. I can still communicate with you by either talking, signing, writing on paper back and forth, and texting etc. I am not limited.

I do whole sale and as well as custom orders for events such as bridal showers, baby showers, etc. Please let me know 4 to 8 weeks ahead so I have the soaps ready for you in time.
